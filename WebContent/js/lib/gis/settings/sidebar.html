About NARWASSCO

Narok Water and Sewerage Services Company Limited (NARWASSCO) is a water service provider in Narok County.

It supplies water in Narok town, Ololulunga town and Kilgoris town.

In Narok town, there are 2 water treatment plants which take water from ENKARE NAROK. Design capacity is 5000m3/day(Central WTP is 1000m3/day, North WTP is 4000m3/day.).

Our transmission line go to FANAKA tank for supply Olopito, London and Majengo.

Ductile Iron main line(DN250mm) supply Lenana, Prison, Oletipis, Total, Pulunga and Mwamba.

In Ololulunga town, there is a WTP which take water from EWASO EGIRO.

Our rising main line go to Ololulunga town tank, Masantare tank and Olon ni tank.

Our network covers Town, Oloirien, Masantare, Olkirai, Catholic church and Senator area.
